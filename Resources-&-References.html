Resources

These resources provide reliable information about plastic pollution, marine ecosystems, and its impact on fishermen and coastal communities:

United Nations Environment Programme (UNEP)

Provides global reports on marine plastic pollution, ocean health, and solutions for waste management. Their materials are useful for understanding the scale of the problem and international efforts to address it.

World Wildlife Fund (WWF)

Offers educational materials, infographics, and case studies on how plastic pollution affects marine life, fisheries, and coastal livelihoods, particularly in Southeast Asia.

 

Department of Environment and Natural Resources (DENR) – Philippines

 

Publishes national reports on marine resources, coastal management, and waste reduction programs in the Philippines.
