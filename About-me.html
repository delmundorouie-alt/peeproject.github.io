About the Author

I am Rouie LJ M. Del Mundo, a student from Applied Mathematics 3-1. I created this website/blog to raise awareness about plastic pollution and its impact on fishermen and coastal communities.

Although I am not a fisherman myself, I deeply care about their struggles and the health of our oceans. Through this project, I wanted to better understand the challenges they face, especially how marine plastic waste affects their livelihood, safety, and daily lives.

As an ordinary citizen, I believe that everyone has a role in protecting our environment and supporting those who depend on it the most. This blog is my way of learning, sharing, and encouraging others to take action in helping our fishermen and keeping our seas clean.

This project was submitted as my final requirement for the subject People, Earth, and Ecosystems. I hope this website helps you understand the issue more clearly and inspires you to care for our oceans and coastal communities.

Thank you for reading.
